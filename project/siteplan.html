Siteplan - Book Haven

ReadiverseHub

Book Haven

Reading is important. Through reading we are able learn new ideas and learn from different stories, perspectives, experiences, downfalls and successes of other people. And to encourage the idea of reading and sharing, ReadiVerseHub shares a small library of books with brief summary and also where one can provide book recommendations to read.

Optional Domain Availability: readiversehub.com

Site Purpose

Readiverhub wesite is to create a place where people can explore books old and new, share their recommendations and connect with others. The site will provide a book catalog, a recommendation form, and a contact page for inquiries.

Scenarios

Scenario 1: A visitor is looking for new books to read. They want to see a collection of books with titles, authors, and genres.

Scenario 2: A visitor has a great book recommendation and wants to share it with others on the website. They need a simple form to submit their recommendation.

Color Schema

The colors selected for this website are meant to reflect the warmth and inviting nature for visitors.

Primary Color: #FFFFF (used for headings, links, and accent buttons)

Secondary Color: #ECEBDE (used for background and content areas)

Other Colors: #CD853F (fonts)

Color Palette: color

Typography

Primary Font: Lato, sans-serif (used for body text, headings, and content)

Secondary Font: Poppins, sans-serif (used for titles and section headings)
